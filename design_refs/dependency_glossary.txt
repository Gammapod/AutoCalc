# Mathematics Tech Tree – Glossary of Nodes

This glossary describes each technology as a capability unlock rather than a school topic.  
Each node represents a structural or conceptual ability that enables downstream mechanics.

---

## 🌱 FOUNDATIONAL PRIMITIVES

### **P0 – Distinguishability**
**Meaning:** Objects can be told apart.  
**Why it matters:** Without distinguishability, there are no "things" to reason about.  
**Unlocks:** Identity, sets, counting.

---

### **P1 – Identity / Equality**
**Meaning:** Recognition of “same vs different.”  
**Why it matters:** Enables comparison, classification, persistence.  
**Unlocks:** Stable objects, equivalence, substitution.

---

### **P2 – Discreteness**
**Meaning:** Reality modeled as separable units.  
**Why it matters:** Counting requires discrete entities.  
**Unlocks:** Natural numbers, finite quantities.

---

### **P3 – Ordering / Succession**
**Meaning:** Concept of “next,” “before,” “after.”  
**Why it matters:** Arithmetic relies on ordered progression.  
**Unlocks:** Counting, comparison, subtraction logic.

---

### **P4 – Iteration / Repetition**
**Meaning:** Applying processes repeatedly.  
**Why it matters:** Arithmetic operations derive from repetition.  
**Unlocks:** Addition, multiplication, exponentiation.

---

## 🏗 ALGEBRAIC STRUCTURES

### **SSet – Set**
**Meaning:** A collection of distinguishable objects.  
**Why it matters:** Foundation of modern mathematics.  
**Unlocks:** Membership, relations, operations.

---

### **SMagma – Magma**
**Meaning:** A set with a binary operation.  
**Why it matters:** Introduces computation/composition.  
**Unlocks:** Raw arithmetic behavior.

---

### **SMonoid – Monoid**
**Meaning:** Associative operation + identity element.  
**Why it matters:** Enables stable iteration.  
**Unlocks:** Addition-like and multiplication-like systems.

---

### **SGroup – Group**
**Meaning:** Monoid + inverses.  
**Why it matters:** Enables cancellation and subtraction.  
**Unlocks:** Negative values, reversible operations.

---

### **SRing – Ring**
**Meaning:** Compatible addition & multiplication structures.  
**Why it matters:** Supports polynomials and factorization.  
**Unlocks:** Algebraic expressions, scaling laws.

---

### **SField – Field**
**Meaning:** Ring + multiplicative inverses.  
**Why it matters:** Enables true division.  
**Unlocks:** Fractions, ratios, continuous scaling.

---

### **SEuclidean – Euclidean Domain**
**Meaning:** Ring supporting division with remainder.  
**Why it matters:** Gateway to number theory.  
**Unlocks:** Modulo arithmetic, GCD, primes ⭐

---

## 🔢 NUMBER SYSTEMS

### **Nℕ – Natural Numbers**
**Meaning:** Positive counting numbers.  
**Why it matters:** Fundamental discrete quantities.  
**Unlocks:** Counting, ordering, basic arithmetic.

---

### **N0 – Zero**
**Meaning:** Additive identity / null quantity.  
**Why it matters:** Enables symmetry & balance.  
**Unlocks:** Integers, algebraic closure pressure.

---

### **Nℤ – Integers**
**Meaning:** Positive + negative whole numbers.  
**Why it matters:** Closure under subtraction.  
**Unlocks:** Signed arithmetic, directionality.

---

### **Nℚ – Rational Numbers**
**Meaning:** Fractions / ratios of integers.  
**Why it matters:** Closure under division.  
**Unlocks:** Proportions, scaling precision.

---

### **Nℝ – Real Numbers**
**Meaning:** Continuous number line.  
**Why it matters:** Measurement & geometry compatibility.  
**Unlocks:** Limits, calculus, trig.

---

### **Nℂ – Complex Numbers**
**Meaning:** Extension including √−1.  
**Why it matters:** Full algebraic closure.  
**Unlocks:** Oscillation, rotation, wave mechanics.

---

## ➕ OPERATIONS

### **O+ – Addition**
**Meaning:** Combining quantities.  
**Why it matters:** Fundamental composition operator.  
**Unlocks:** Linear growth, accumulation.

---

### **O− – Subtraction**
**Meaning:** Inverse of addition / difference.  
**Why it matters:** Enables removal & balancing.  
**Unlocks:** Negative reasoning, cancellation.

---

### **O× – Multiplication**
**Meaning:** Repeated addition / scaling.  
**Why it matters:** Introduces proportional change.  
**Unlocks:** Area models, combinatorics.

---

### **O÷ – True Division**
**Meaning:** Inverse of multiplication.  
**Why it matters:** Enables ratios & fractions.  
**Unlocks:** Continuous scaling.

---

### **O% – Division with Remainder (Modulo)**
**Meaning:** Residual after division.  
**Why it matters:** Structural shift into constraints & cycles ⭐  
**Unlocks:** Modular arithmetic, residue classes, number theory.

---

### **O^ – Exponentiation**
**Meaning:** Repeated multiplication.  
**Why it matters:** Nonlinear growth dynamics.  
**Unlocks:** Exponential systems, logs.

---

### **O√ – Roots**
**Meaning:** Inverse of exponentiation.  
**Why it matters:** Closure pressure for ℝ → ℂ.  
**Unlocks:** Irrationals, polynomial solutions.

---

## 🔣 REPRESENTATION SYSTEMS (Interface Technologies)

### **Rint – Integer Notation**
**Meaning:** Symbolic compression of discrete quantities.  
**Unlocks:** Efficient arithmetic manipulation.

---

### **Rfrac – Fraction Notation**
**Meaning:** Explicit ratio representation.  
**Unlocks:** Rational intuition & simplification.

---

### **Rdecimal – Decimal System**
**Meaning:** Positional base-10 encoding.  
**Unlocks:** Compactness, estimation, scaling usability.

---

### **Rscientific – Scientific Notation**
**Meaning:** Magnitude compression.  
**Unlocks:** Extreme scale management.

---

### **Rsymbolic – Symbolic Algebra**
**Meaning:** Variables & expressions.  
**Unlocks:** Generalized reasoning.

---

## 📈 FUNCTION TECHNOLOGIES

### **Fid – Identity Function**
**Meaning:** Output equals input.  
**Why it matters:** Neutral baseline operator.

---

### **Flin – Linear Functions**
**Meaning:** Addition + multiplication structure.  
**Unlocks:** Constant-rate dynamics.

---

### **Fpoly – Polynomial Functions**
**Meaning:** Ring-based expressions.  
**Unlocks:** Curvature, roots, factorization.

---

### **Fexp – Exponential Functions**
**Meaning:** Power-based growth.  
**Unlocks:** Explosive dynamics.

---

### **Flog – Logarithmic Functions**
**Meaning:** Inverse of exponentiation.  
**Unlocks:** Scale compression & inversion.

---

### **Ftrig – Trigonometric Functions**
**Meaning:** Periodic / rotational functions.  
**Why it matters:** Requires ℝ + geometry ⭐  
**Unlocks:** Oscillation, cycles, waves.

---

### **Fcalc – Calculus Operators**
**Meaning:** Limits, derivatives, integrals.  
**Unlocks:** Continuous change mechanics.

---

# 🎮 Design Insight

Nodes differ in *what kind of reasoning becomes possible*:

Arithmetic → Scaling → Constraints → Cycles → Continuity → Oscillation → Dynamics

Certain unlocks (notably **SEuclidean / O%**) produce disproportionate gameplay richness.
